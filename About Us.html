ABOUT US

Founded in 1998 by the talented baker John Thompson, John's Bakery began as a small local store in a busy neighbourhood. John spent his life building meals that combined standard
techniques with creative flavours in order to achieve his dream of feeding the locals with fresh,
fresh-baked bread and cookies, pastries along with coffee. His famous bread quickly grew fame and attracted both loyal clients and hungry newcomers. Johns began as a small neighbourhood bakery and has since expanded to become a
well-liked local institution known for its welcoming atmosphere and tasty goodies. These days, John's Bakery is about more than just goods; it's about community,
acting as a place where people can enjoy delicious food and make lifelong memories!
